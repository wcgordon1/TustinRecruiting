The role of a Property Manager in Ashland, NE, offers a unique opportunity for an experienced professional to oversee and enhance the management of retail properties within a vibrant community. This position is ideal for a candidate who thrives in a dynamic and challenging environment, with a focus on tenant relations, budgeting, and maintenance coordination. The successful applicant will work closely with senior management, tenants, and vendors to ensure the seamless operation of multiple retail properties. This position requires a strategic thinker with strong financial acumen and the ability to foster positive tenant relationships. 

## Key Responsibilities

- Manage day-to-day operations of retail properties, ensuring smooth and efficient functioning.
- Develop and execute comprehensive property budgets and financial plans to optimize profitability.
- Oversee tenant relations, addressing inquiries, disputes, and ensuring lease compliance.
- Coordinate property maintenance and vendor management to deliver high-quality service.
- Negotiate and manage service contracts with a focus on cost-effectiveness.
- Lead leasing efforts and support marketing activities to maximize occupancy rates.
- Ensure compliance with local regulations and company policies, maintaining legal and operational standards.
- Monitor rent collections and address delinquencies promptly to maintain cash flow.
- Prepare monthly and quarterly reports for senior management, providing insights into property performance.
- Mentor and guide junior team members to foster professional growth within the team.
- Conduct regular property inspections to ensure safety standards are met and address any issues promptly.
- Collaborate with local community leaders and stakeholders to enhance the property’s presence in Ashland.
- Implement sustainable practices to improve property efficiency and reduce environmental impact.

## Required Qualifications

- Proven experience in property management with a strong emphasis on retail properties.
- Demonstrated expertise in budgeting and financial planning, critical for effective property management.
- Excellent leadership and communication skills to guide teams and foster positive tenant relations.
- Proficiency in property management software and Microsoft Excel, essential for managing property data.
- Ability to manage multiple properties and prioritize tasks effectively in a fast-paced environment.
- Broker’s license preferred but not required, though a commitment to obtaining one is advantageous.
- 5-8 years of experience required in Property Management within an Operations setting.
- Familiarity with the Ashland, NE, real estate market and local business community.
- Strong network with local vendors and service providers in Ashland to facilitate efficient operations.

## Compensation & Benefits

- Competitive salary range: $81,300 - $86,300, depending on experience and qualifications.
- Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage to ensure well-being.
- Generous paid time off and holidays to support work-life balance.
- Career advancement opportunities within a growing organization.
- Ongoing training and certifications to enhance professional development and expertise.

This position is a compelling opportunity for a dedicated Property Manager to make a significant impact on the management and success of retail properties in Ashland, NE.
