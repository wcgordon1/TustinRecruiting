Join a dynamic team in Papillion, NE, as a Retail Property Analyst. This role offers the opportunity to impact retail leasing strategies through detailed financial performance analysis, budgeting, and market research. You'll work with industry professionals to develop strategies that drive profitability for retail properties.

## Key Responsibilities
- Analyze financial performance of retail properties, emphasizing financial modeling and reporting.
- Prepare detailed reports on property budgets, expenses, and profitability.
- Provide market research and competitor analysis to inform leasing strategies.
- Develop financial models to evaluate leasing scenarios.
- Assist property managers with budget preparation and expense tracking.
- Monitor portfolio performance and identify opportunities for improvement.
- Maintain databases of lease terms, tenant information, and market data.
- Support due diligence for acquisitions or dispositions of properties.
- Present insights and recommendations to senior management.

## Required Qualifications
- Strong analytical and financial modeling skills.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and data visualization tools.
- Knowledge of commercial property management practices.
- 5-7 years of experience required for Finance work in Operations setting.
- Excellent communication and presentation abilities.
- Ability to adapt to Papillion's growing retail market.
- Familiarity with local commercial real estate trends.

## Compensation & Benefits
- Competitive salary range: $75,000-$95,000 depending on experience.
- Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Career advancement opportunities.
- Ongoing training and certifications.
